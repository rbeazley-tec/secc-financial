Sustainability Analysis

What occupancy rate does SECC need?

Breakeven occupancy depends on which cost base is used. With the Diocese funding the ED compensation package, SECC's current operations break even near 35%. Full self-sufficiency — covering all staff including the ED — requires approximately 55%, a level achieved only in 2008–2010.

✓ Current operations are near breakeven. Excluding ED compensation (Diocese-funded), SECC breaks even at approximately 34.9% occupancy. The center achieved 40.6% in 2025 — operating slightly above this threshold in terms of cost coverage, though the full budget remains in deficit.
→ Full self-sufficiency requires 55.1% occupancy — covering all staff including ED, at current billing rates of ~$145/guest-night. This level has not been achieved since 2008–2010. The structural gap between achievable occupancy (~40–50%) and full self-sufficiency represents the Diocese's ongoing investment in SECC's mission.
